Become smarter by taking notes
Jul 9, 2018
Taking notes, making lists is an important part of management, especially if you are looking to work on a very big project. In the same light, I have seen many such tools but only a few have gone out of their way to provide a decent experience along. If you are a programmer, then GitHub Gists are worth a look. As a project manager, I use trello alot. I recommend everybody to start using it as I couldnt think of a specific task that doesnt need good management. Another alternative, an inferior to trello is the Google Keep. I used it a year ago and forgot. I kept my super secret ideas in it. About a month ago I used it again. Those were some good ideas and thanks to Keep for saving them. Its materialistic interface is worth a while. Trello is superior a platform that really loves its customers. I invited alot of my friends to Trello, and they were generous enough to offer me their premium package for free.
Amarnath Pilgrimage
Apr 30, 2018
I returned from my 4th pilgrimage to the Amarnath Cave, the cave of immortal being. Its in Kashmir, one of the disturbed places in India. Thanks to the CRPF, there was a guard every 200m - 600m in our 5 day bus trip. Past few years had some incidents of the locals smashing the buses of the pilgrims. Aside from scaring you for your life, the place is near heaven. You might notice how behind (technologically) some of the places are, and those were the most beautiful ones too. I guess I can go as far ahead and say that people havent ruined the beauty of the nature there. Still, the police did manage to recieve a few threats from the "terrorists". Time might not really have the best plans for such a beautiful place which exists because of less-to-no advancements of humans. I think it should be a duty of ours to preserve it for the future.
